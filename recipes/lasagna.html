Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, garfield's favourite meal

Pasta, sauce and meat. All layered on top of each other and baked, not much else to say.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Mince Meat
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Place a sheet of pasta down
  2. Spread a beef mince and sauce mixture on top
  3. Repeat until you are satisfied with the height of lasagna
  4. Place a final sheet of pasta on top
  5. Bake in oven
  6. Do not contact me if I have missed some steps and your dish turns out horrible
